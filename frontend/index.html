Quick setup guide

Follow these steps once, then generate a timetable.

  1. Check you have added all teachers and their basic details.
  2. Add all rooms with correct capacity and type (lecture / lab / etc.).
  3. Create your classes (program, year, section, class size).
  4. Add subjects and mark any special room type they need.
  5. Assign subjects to teachers and classes (hours per week).
  6. Define the school day & periods under “Day & Periods”.
  7. Go to Generate Timetable and click “Generate Timetable”.
